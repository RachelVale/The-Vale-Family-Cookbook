Fish Tacos

Pretend this is fish

Fish Tacos with onion and cilantro

Ingredients

  • 2 filets of white fish
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper
  • 12 corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Add oil to pan over medium heat
  2. Add fish to pan
  3. Season fish with spices
  4. Roughly chop cilantro
  5. Quarter lime
  6. Cook fish 5 minutes per side
  7. Shred fish with a fork
  8. Add fish to tortillas
  9. Garnish with cilantro, and lime
