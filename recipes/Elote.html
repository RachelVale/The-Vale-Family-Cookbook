Elote En Vaso

Street corn in a cup, extra spicy

Ingredients

  • 3 ears of sweet corn
  • 2 habanero peppers
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 1 1/2 Cup of cotija
  • 1/4 Cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Cup of sour cream
  • 1 lime
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 420*F
  2. Shuck corn and remove it from the cob
  3. Dice peppers
  4. Roughly chop cilantro
  5. Quarter lime
  6. Combine corn, peppers, cotija, mayonnaise, sour cream, and spices in baking pan
  7. Bake for 25 minutes
  8. Serve in cups
  9. Garnish with cheese, cilantro, and lime
